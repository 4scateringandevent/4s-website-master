Welcome to

4S Catering & Events.

4S Catering and Events is one of the trusted names in catering and event services in Thiruvananthapuram. What started as a dedicated catering service has now grown into a complete event solutions company offering catering, event management, stage decoration, makeup services, accommodation arrangements, and photography & videography services.

More than 15 years of experience, a passionate team, and a commitment to quality, we ensure every event is executed with perfection and attention to detail. Whether it’s a wedding, engagement, birthday celebration, corporate gathering, or private function, we handle every aspect professionally so our clients can enjoy their moments stress-free.

At 4S Catering and Events, we combine taste, creativity, hospitality, and flawless planning to deliver experiences that leave lasting impressions.

Our Journey

From Passionate Catering to Complete Event Excellence

4S Catering and Events was founded with a simple vision — to provide exceptional food and unforgettable hospitality experiences. Under the leadership and dedication of Mr. Sathish, the company quickly earned the trust and appreciation of clients through quality service, delicious cuisine, and professional event execution.

Over the years, 4S expanded beyond catering and evolved into a complete event management company. Today, we proudly provide end-to-end event solutions that cover every important aspect of an event under one roof.

Our journey has been built on hard work, customer satisfaction, innovation, and a passion for creating memorable celebrations. Every event we manage reflects our commitment to excellence and personalized service.
